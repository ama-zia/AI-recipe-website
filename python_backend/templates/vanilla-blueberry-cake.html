Fluffy Vanilla Blueberry Layer Cake

There's something quietly irresistible about a good vanilla blueberry cake—something that feels nostalgic and elevated all at once. The fluffy layers, the gentle sweetness of the vanilla sponge, and the vibrant bursts of blueberries create a balance that never feels too rich or too plain. This is the kind of cake that wins over both those who say “I’m not really a dessert person” and those who unapologetically ask for a second slice.

What sets this version apart is the layering of flavors and textures: a delicate cake crumb, a bright homemade blueberry compote (no soggy berries here), and a whipped cream cheese frosting that’s soft enough to melt on your tongue, but structured enough to stack. It’s easy to mess up a layer cake, but with a little patience—and the tips in this recipe—you’ll get picture-perfect slices every time.

Serving size: 10–12 slices

Baking time: 25–30 minutes at 175°C / 350°F

Cooling time: 10 minutes in pan, then cool completely on rack

What to Expect

  • Moist vanilla sponge with bursts of blueberries
  • Sweet-tart blueberry compote (no soggy berries)
  • Light, fluffy vanilla whipped cream cheese frosting
  • Bakery-worthy layering — home baker-friendly

Ingredients

Vanilla Cake (3 layers, 8-inch pans):

  • 2 ½ cups (310g) cake flour (or all-purpose, sifted well)
  • 2 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup (170g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ¾ cups (350g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg whites (room temp)
  • 1 tbsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk (room temp)
  • ½ cup (120g) sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 1 ½ cups blueberries (tossed in 1 tbsp flour)

Blueberry Compote (for layering or topping):

  • 2 cups blueberries (fresh or frozen)
  • ⅓ cup (65g) sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water (slurry)
  • Optional: ½ tsp vanilla extract

Vanilla Whipped Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 225g (8 oz) cream cheese, cold
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups (180g) powdered sugar, sifted
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream, very cold

Step-by-Step Method

Step 1: Bake the Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C / 350°F. Grease and line three 8-inch pans.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until pale and fluffy (3–5 mins).
  4. Add egg whites gradually, beating well. Add vanilla.
  5. Mix in sour cream.
  6. Add dry ingredients in 3 parts, alternating with buttermilk. Don’t overmix.
  7. Fold in floured blueberries gently.
  8. Divide into pans. Bake 25–30 mins or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Cool in pans 10 mins, then transfer to racks. Wrap and chill for easy handling.

Step 2: Make Blueberry Compote

  1. In a saucepan, cook blueberries, sugar, and lemon juice over medium heat.
  2. Once juicy and bubbling, stir in cornstarch slurry. Cook until thick.
  3. Stir in vanilla. Cool completely.

Step 3: Make the Frosting

  1. Beat cream cheese until smooth (cold is fine). Add butter, beat until fluffy.
  2. Add powdered sugar and vanilla.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip cream until soft peaks.
  4. Gently fold whipped cream into cream cheese mixture until fluffy and spreadable.
