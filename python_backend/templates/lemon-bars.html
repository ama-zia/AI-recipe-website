Lemon Bars

Lemon bars have a way of stealing the show at any gathering — they balance tangy citrus with rich, buttery crust in a way that feels both indulgent and refreshing. The key to mastering them lies in timing and texture. You want that crust firm enough to hold the luscious filling without becoming dry, and a lemon layer that’s just set, never rubbery. Don’t be tempted to crank up the heat — a slow bake keeps the eggs tender and the flavor bright. Zesting fresh lemons makes a world of difference, and using parchment helps ensure clean removal from the pan. Whether you like them mouth-puckeringly tart or just sweet enough, lemon bars can be easily adjusted to taste.

Serving size: 12 bars

Bake time: 15–18 minutes (crust) + 20–25 minutes (filling)

Temperature: 175°C / 350°F (crust), 160°C / 325°F (filling)

Cooling time: Cool at room temp, then chill for 2 hours

Shortbread Crust:

  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup (30g) powdered sugar
  • ½ cup (115g) cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • Zest of ½ lemon

Crust Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C / 350°F.
  2. Mix flour, powdered sugar, salt, and lemon zest in a bowl.
  3. Cut in cold butter with fork or fingers until crumbly.
  4. Press evenly into a parchment-lined baking tin.
  5. Bake for 15–18 minutes until edges are lightly golden.

Lemon Filling:

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • ½ cup (120ml) fresh lemon juice (2–3 lemons)
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp (30g) melted unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Filling & Baking

  1. Whisk sugar and flour in a bowl to prevent clumps.
  2. Add eggs, lemon juice, zest, melted butter, and salt. Whisk until smooth (don’t overwhisk).
  3. Pour over warm baked crust.
  4. Bake at 160°C / 325°F for 20–25 minutes until center is just set with a slight jiggle.
  5. Cool completely at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

To Serve

  1. Dust generously with powdered sugar before serving.
  2. For neat slices, wipe your knife clean between each cut.

Bright, tangy, and buttery — the ultimate lemony treat
