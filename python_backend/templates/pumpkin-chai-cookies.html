Pumpkin Chai Cookies

There’s something quietly confident about these Pumpkin Chai Cookies. The dough is fragrant with warming spices like cardamom and cinnamon, and the pumpkin gives them a soft, chewy center that stays tender for days. These aren’t showy cookies—they don’t need to be. They shine through texture and balance, perfect for crisp afternoons or low-key evenings.

The key to mastering them lies in the browning of the butter and the resting time for the dough. Don’t rush the chill—it helps the flavors deepen and the texture firm up just enough. Serving Size: Makes 10–12 cookies

Bake Time: 10–12 minutes

Oven Temperature: 175°C / 350°F

Cool Time: 5 minutes on tray, then transfer to rack

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ginger
  • ½ tsp cardamom
  • ¼ tsp cloves
  • ¼ tsp allspice
  • OR 1½ tsp pumpkin spice + ½ tsp chai spice blend

Wet Ingredients:

  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, browned
  • ½ cup (100g) brown sugar
  • ¼ cup (50g) white sugar
  • ⅓ cup (80g) pumpkin puree (not too watery)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Spiced Sugar Coating:

  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch of cardamom

Method

1. Brown the Butter

  1. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Let it bubble and foam until golden brown and nutty-smelling.
  3. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.

2. Mix the Dough

  1. In a bowl, whisk browned butter with both sugars.
  2. Stir in pumpkin puree, egg yolk, and vanilla.
  3. In another bowl, mix all dry ingredients together.
  4. Fold dry into wet with a spatula until just combined.
  5. Chill dough for 30–60 minutes (important for chewiness).

3. Shape and Coat

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C / 350°F.
  2. Mix spiced sugar in a small bowl.
  3. Scoop chilled dough into 1.5 tbsp balls, roll in sugar.
  4. Place on parchment-lined tray, spaced apart.

4. Bake!

  1. Bake for 10–12 minutes until edges are set and tops are crackly.
  2. At 8-minute mark, bang tray gently for chewier centers.
  3. Cool on tray for 5 minutes, then transfer to a rack.

Warm, chewy, and spiced to perfection
