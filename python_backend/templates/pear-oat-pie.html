Pear Oat Pie

Pear Oat Pie is one of those timeless desserts that brings together the rustic comfort of baked fruit and the warmth of a buttery oat topping. It’s a bake that doesn’t try too hard, yet it delivers flavor and texture that feel effortless but impressive. The juicy softness of ripe pears contrasts beautifully with the crisp oat crumble, while the flaky crust gives the whole thing structure and balance.

To really ace this dessert, start with pears that are ripe but still firm—this keeps the filling from becoming mushy. Keep your butter cold for both the crust and crumble; it’s key to getting those tender layers and crunchy golden topping. Letting the pie cool is non-negotiable—it gives the filling time to set, so every slice holds its shape.

Serving Size: 8 slices

Bake Time: 45–55 minutes at 190°C / 375°F

Cool Time: At least 1 hour before serving

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1¼ cups (155g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (115g) cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 3–4 tbsp ice water
  • Optional: Store-bought pie shell

For the Pear Filling:

  • 5–6 firm ripe pears, peeled, cored, sliced ½ cm thick
  • ¼ cup (50g) brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt

For the Crisp Oat Crumble:

  • ¾ cup (75g) rolled oats
  • ½ cup (60g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (100g) brown sugar
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch salt
  • ½ cup (115g) cold butter, cubed

Method

1. Prepare the Crust

  1. Mix flour, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Cut in cold butter until mixture looks like crumbs.
  3. Add ice water one tablespoon at a time until dough forms.
  4. Shape into a disc, wrap, and chill 30 mins.
  5. Roll and press into 9" pie dish. Prick with fork. Chill again.
  6. Optional: Blind bake at 190°C / 375°F for 10 mins.

2. Make the Filling

  1. Toss pear slices with sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  2. Let sit while prepping the crumble — juices will release.

3. Make the Crumble

  1. Mix oats, flour, sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Rub in cold butter until it forms chunky crumbs.

4. Assemble & Bake

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C / 375°F.
  2. Pour pear filling into chilled crust (include juices).
  3. Top with oat crumble evenly.
  4. Bake 45–55 mins until golden and bubbly.
  5. If crumble browns too fast, tent with foil.

5. Cool & Serve

  1. Cool for at least 1 hour so filling sets.
  2. Serve warm with whipped cream, vanilla ice cream, or caramel drizzle.
