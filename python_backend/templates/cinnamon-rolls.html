Cream Cheese Cinnamon Rolls

These cream cheese cinnamon rolls strike the perfect balance between rich, tender dough and a swirl of sweet cinnamon sugar, finished off with a luscious cream cheese frosting that clings to every soft, warm layer. Getting these rolls right is all about paying attention to the dough texture — smooth, slightly tacky, never dry — and letting it rise patiently until fully doubled. Spreading the filling evenly and rolling with a steady hand guarantees those neat, bakery-style spirals. The frosting ties everything together with a subtle tang that cuts the sweetness beautifully. Serving Size: Makes 12 rolls
Baking Time: 20–25 minutes
Temperature: 175°C (350°F)
Cooling Time: 10–15 minutes before frosting

Ingredients

Dough:

  • ¾ cup (180ml) whole milk, warm (~40°C or 105°F)
  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temp
  • ¼ cup (60g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 ¾ cups (470g) all-purpose flour (plus extra if needed)
  • ¾ tsp salt

Filling:

  • ⅔ cup (130g) brown sugar (light or dark)
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • ¼ cup (60g) unsalted butter, softened
  • Optional: pinch of nutmeg or splash of vanilla

Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 115g (½ cup) cream cheese, room temp
  • ¼ cup (60g) unsalted butter, room temp
  • 1 ½ cups (180g) powdered sugar, sifted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: splash of milk or cream to thin

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Make the Dough

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let sit 5–10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add eggs, melted butter, and salt. Mix gently.
  3. Add flour gradually. Mix until soft dough forms.
  4. Knead 5–8 minutes until smooth and slightly tacky.
  5. Transfer to a greased bowl, cover, and rise 1.5–2 hours until doubled in size.

Step 2: Fill & Roll

  1. Roll dough into a 16x12 inch rectangle.
  2. Spread softened butter over the dough.
  3. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon, then sprinkle on top.
  4. Roll tightly from the long side into a log.
  5. Slice into 12 even pieces.

Step 3: Second Rise

  1. Arrange rolls in a buttered 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Cover loosely and rise 30–45 minutes until puffy.

Step 4: Bake & Frost

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Bake 20–25 minutes until golden and set in the center.
  3. Cool for 10–15 minutes before spreading cream cheese frosting generously over the rolls.
