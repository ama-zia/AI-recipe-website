Japanese Fluffy Pancakes

Japanese fluffy pancakes aren't your typical breakfast stack. They're airy, souffle-like, and a little technical, but that's part of what makes them satisfying to master. If you've ever wondered how to get that dramatic rise and custard-soft texture, the secret is in gentle mixing, low heat, and a little patience.

These pancakes take time, but they reward you with something closer to a plated dessert than a diner breakfast. Don't rush it—heat too high or batter too thin, and you'll end up with collapsed domes or dry insides. Follow the method carefully and you'll get jiggly, cloud-like pancakes with golden edges and a delicately sweet flavor.

Serving Size: Makes 4–6 mini pancakes

Cooking Time: 6–7 minutes on one side, 4–5 minutes on the other

Temperature: Low stovetop heat (approx. 120°C / 250°F)

Cooling Time: Optional, 1 minute uncovered in pan after removing molds

Ingredients

For the Pancakes:

  • ¾ cup (90g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup (120g) plain yogurt
  • 3 tbsp milk (more if needed)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp melted butter (plus more for pan)
  • Optional: ½ tsp vanilla bean paste

Strawberry Compote:

  • 1 cup (150g) fresh or frozen strawberries, halved
  • 1 ½ tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Optional: drop of vanilla

Vanilla Cream:

  • ½ cup (120ml) heavy cream, very cold
  • 1 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract or paste

Step-by-Step Method

Step 1: Make the Pancake Batter

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, milk, egg, vanilla, melted butter.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Stir until just smooth. Don’t overmix.
  4. Batter should be thick and scoopable. Add 1 tsp milk if too thick.

Step 2: Cook Fluffy Pancakes

  1. Heat a nonstick pan on low heat and lightly butter it.
  2. Use metal ring molds (or foil ones). Grease inside of rings.
  3. Scoop batter into molds (¾ full). Cover with lid.
  4. Cook for 6–7 mins on low heat until bubbles form and sides are set.
  5. Flip gently with spatula and tongs. Cook other side 4–5 mins.
  6. Remove molds carefully. Let pancakes cook uncovered for 1 more minute.

Step 3: Make Strawberry Compote

  1. In a small pan, add strawberries, sugar, lemon juice.
  2. Cook on medium heat for 5–7 mins until soft and syrupy.
  3. Let cool slightly — warm and spoonable is perfect.

Step 4: Make Vanilla Cream

  1. Whip cold cream, vanilla, and powdered sugar until soft peaks form.
  2. Keep chilled until ready to serve with pancakes.
