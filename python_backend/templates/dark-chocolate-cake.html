Dark Chocolate Cherry Cake

This Dark Chocolate Cherry Cake brings together the deep richness of cocoa and the tart brightness of cherries in a way that feels indulgent but still balanced. The cake layers are moist and deeply chocolatey, enhanced with coffee for depth, while the cherry compote adds a fresh pop of fruitiness that cuts through the richness. The ganache frosting pulls everything together with its smooth texture and dark, glossy finish. To get this cake just right, make sure to chill your layers before assembling to avoid sliding or uneven stacking. Don't skip blooming the cocoa in hot coffee — it's key to unlocking maximum chocolate flavor. Serving Size: Serves 10–12

Bake Time: 25–30 minutes

Oven Temperature: 175°C / 350°F

Cooling Time: 1 hour (before layering)

Ingredients

Chocolate Cake (3 layers, 8-inch pans):

  • 1 ¾ cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup (65g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs (room temp)
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk (room temp)
  • ½ cup (120ml) neutral oil
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) hot strong coffee

Cherry Compote Filling:

  • 2 cups (300g) pitted cherries
  • ¼ cup (50g) sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water
  • Optional: 1–2 tbsp kirsch

Fluffy Dark Chocolate Ganache Frosting:

  • 300g dark chocolate (60–70%)
  • 1 ½ cups (360ml) heavy cream
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: 1 tbsp butter for shine

Step-by-Step Directions

Step 1: Make the Chocolate Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C / 350°F. Grease and line pans.
  2. Mix flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, soda, salt.
  3. Add eggs, buttermilk, oil, vanilla – mix well.
  4. Slowly pour in hot coffee. Batter will be thin.
  5. Divide into pans. Bake 25–30 mins.
  6. Cool, wrap in plastic, and chill before layering.
