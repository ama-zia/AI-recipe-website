Apple Cake

Apple cake is one of those cozy, familiar desserts that feels like home in every bite. It’s tender, moist, and loaded with juicy apple pieces that almost melt into the soft crumb. The trick to nailing this recipe is using crisp, tart apples like Granny Smiths or Pink Ladies — they hold their shape and give a perfect balance to the sweetness. Don’t skip the cinnamon, either. It adds warmth that transforms simple ingredients into something special.

Serving Size: 9 slices

Baking Time: 28–35 minutes

Oven Temperature: 175°C / 350°F

Cooling Time: 10 minutes in pan before transferring to wire rack

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups (200g) oat flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg (optional)
  • ½ cup (120ml) neutral oil (sunflower or light olive oil)
  • ½ cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temp
  • ½ cup (120g) Greek yogurt or thick plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 medium apples, peeled and cubed (~1½ cups)

Optional Cinnamon Sugar Topping:

  • 1 tbsp sugar + ½ tsp cinnamon (mixed)
  • Sprinkle on top before baking for a sweet crust

Step-by-Step Method

Step 1: Prep

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C / 350°F.
  2. Grease and line an 8x8 inch or round cake pan with parchment paper.

Step 2: Mix Batter

  1. In one bowl, whisk: oat flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg.
  2. In another large bowl, whisk oil and brown sugar until blended.
  3. Add eggs one at a time. Stir in yogurt and vanilla.
  4. Fold dry ingredients into wet — don’t overmix.
  5. Gently fold in apple cubes.

Step 3: Bake

  1. Pour batter into prepared pan and spread evenly.
  2. Optional: sprinkle with cinnamon sugar topping.
  3. Bake 28–35 minutes, until golden and a toothpick comes out clean or with moist crumbs.
  4. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire rack.

Warm hugs in cake form — enjoy with tea or coffee
