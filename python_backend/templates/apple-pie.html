Apple Crumble Pie

Apple Crumble Pie is one of those desserts that manages to strike the perfect balance between rustic comfort and impressive presentation. It's not about perfection here—it's about texture, warmth, and a satisfying crunch layered over a soft, spiced apple filling. If you want to truly master this pie, use firm tart apples like Granny Smiths and don’t skip resting the filling before baking; it draws out juices for a rich, caramel-like flavor. Make sure your crumble topping stays chilled before baking so it crisps up properly. You can even blind-bake the crust for a crisper base if you're worried about sogginess.

Serving Size: 8 slices

Baking Temperature: 190°C / 375°F

Baking Time: 45–55 minutes

Cooling Time: At least 1 hour before slicing

Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 6–7 medium Granny Smith apples (1.2kg / 2.5 lbs), peeled and sliced
  • ½ cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

For the Pie Crust:

  • 1 9-inch pie crust, chilled and ready
  • Optional: blind-bake at 190°C / 375°F for 8–10 min

For the Crumble Topping:

  • ¾ cup (90g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (100g) brown sugar
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ⅓ cup (75g) unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • ½ cup (50g) rolled oats
  • Optional: ¼ cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Step-by-Step Method

Step 1: Prep the Apple Filling

  1. Peel, core, and thinly slice the apples.
  2. Toss in a large bowl with sugars, cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, flour, vanilla, and salt.
  3. Let sit for 10–15 minutes to draw out juices.

Step 2: Make the Crumble

  1. In a medium bowl, mix flour, brown sugar, and cinnamon.
  2. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture looks like coarse crumbs.
  3. Stir in oats and nuts if using. Chill if your kitchen is warm.

Step 3: Assemble the Pie

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C / 375°F.
  2. Place the crust in your pie dish, fluting the edges if desired.
  3. Fill with the apple mixture (don’t add all the juices).
  4. Top with crumble, pressing lightly so it sticks.

Step 4: Bake

  1. Bake for 45–55 minutes until golden brown and bubbling.
  2. If the crust or topping browns too fast, cover loosely with foil.
  3. Cool for at least 1 hour before slicing.
