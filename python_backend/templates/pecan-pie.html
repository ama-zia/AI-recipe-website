Pecan Pie

Pecan pie is one of those desserts that looks humble but delivers unforgettable flavor in every bite. With its rich, buttery filling and the deep caramel tones of toasted pecans, it's a dessert that quietly steals the show on any table, not just during the holidays. The key to mastering pecan pie lies in nailing the balance — the filling should be set but not rubbery, sweet but not cloying, with the pecans evenly distributed so each slice has crunch and character. Using toasted pecans makes a surprising difference in flavor depth, so don’t skip that step. Let the pie cool properly — this helps the filling firm up and slice cleanly. Serving size: 8 slices

Baking temperature: 175°C / 350°F

Baking time: 45-55 minutes

Cooling time: 3-4 hours

Pie Crust Ingredients:

  • 1¼ cups (155g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ½ cup (115g) cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 2-4 tbsp ice water

Shortcut: Use 1 sheet of ready-made shortcrust pastry!

Crust Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut in cold butter using your fingertips or fork until it resembles breadcrumbs with pea-sized bits.
  3. Add ice water 1 tbsp at a time, mixing until dough just comes together.
  4. Flatten into a disc, wrap, and chill for 30-45 minutes.

Pecan Filling Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (200g) brown sugar
  • ¼ cup (60ml) maple syrup or golden syrup
  • ¼ cup (60ml) honey
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • ¼ cup (60g) melted unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1½ cups (160g) toasted pecan halves

Pie Assembly & Baking

  1. Preheat: Set oven to 175°C / 350°F.
  2. Prep Crust: Roll dough on a floured surface. Fit into a 9-inch pie dish, trim, and crimp the edges.
  3. Prick the base with a fork and freeze for 10 minutes.
  4. (Optional) Blind bake for 10 minutes for extra crispness.
  5. Make Filling: In a bowl, whisk sugar, syrup, honey, melted butter, vanilla, salt, flour, and eggs.
  6. Stir in half the pecans.
  7. Assemble: Pour filling into crust and arrange the rest of the pecans neatly on top (optional but pretty!).
  8. Bake: Bake 45-55 minutes until edges are set and center is slightly jiggly.
  9. Cover loosely with foil at 30 minutes if browning too fast.
  10. Cool: Let pie cool completely (about 3-4 hours) before slicing.

Nutty, buttery, and rich — this pecan pie is a timeless treat
